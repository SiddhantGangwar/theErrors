ABOUT US

India In-Sight is an amazing initiative from our side to help visitors navigate many wonderful places, both famous and local , very easily. We provide several services and information which makes it easier for the user to grasp the information and takes away a lot of time and hastle. It started as a project to help others and we hope it ends up helping you guys too. India In-Sight is alive due to the efforts of a few students of Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, namely Shubham Marathe, Garima Yadav, Siddhant Gangwar and Archit Bhatnagar.

AIM

Our AIM with this website is to make navigation of certain cities and places a lot easier for the purpose of travelling. We believe that for experiencing true wonder , eleminating basic hurdles is essential. With the help of India In-Sight , unlock the true potential of your trip and vacation without any worries. When you have all essential services in one place , take a sip of that cucumber water and chill !

WORKING PRINCIPLE

India In-Sight acts like an essential tool to any person visiting several sites in India. It provides the user with many important links and services which one might need during their adventurous journey. You can rest assured as imformation like locations of the sites, food and local delicacies, hotels and landmarks etc are easily accessible at one place. In case of emergencies you can contact us for help and someone will surely come up to respond.
